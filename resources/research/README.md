# Research Materials and References

This directory contains recommended research materials, papers, and resources for the program.

## Academic Papers and Whitepapers

**Adversarial Examples** - Goodfellow et al.
- Foundational paper on adversarial examples
- URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1312.6199

**FGSM Attack** - Fast Gradient Sign Method
- Paper on evasion attacks using gradient-based methods
- URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1412.6572

**Data Poisoning** - Backdoor Attacks
- Research on data poisoning attacks
- URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1206.6389

- URL: https://www.nist.gov/cyberframework

**NIST AI Risk Management Framework** - AI-specific guidance
- Framework for managing risks in AI systems
- URL: https://www.nist.gov/publications/artificial-intelligence-risk-management-framework

**EU AI Act** - European Union Regulation
- Regulatory framework for AI in the European Union
