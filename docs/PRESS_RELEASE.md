## ICDFA Launches World-Class AI Cybersecurity Program Crafted by Industry Veteran Aminu Idris

**[CITY, STATE] – January 17, 2026** – The International Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics Academy (ICDFA) today announced the launch of its groundbreaking 12-month intensive program, **Advanced AI in Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics**. This comprehensive curriculum, meticulously developed by renowned cybersecurity educator and industry veteran Aminu Idris, AMCPN, is designed to address the critical global shortage of professionals skilled in AI-powered cyber defense and forensic analysis.

In an era where AI-driven cyberattacks are becoming increasingly sophisticated, the ICDFA program provides a production-ready, expert-led educational framework. The curriculum moves beyond theoretical concepts to provide deep, hands-on training across 12 integrated labs, covering everything from AI-powered threat intelligence to advanced adversarial attack simulation and AI security validation.

This program distinguishes itself by being entirely human-crafted and expert-driven. Under the leadership of Aminu Idris, the Founder and Commandant of ICDFA, every module, lab, and assessment has been developed to reflect real-world challenges and industry best practices. This ensures that graduates are equipped with practical, battle-tested skills, not just automated or theoretical knowledge.

"We are facing a new frontier in cyber warfare, and traditional training programs are failing to keep pace," said Aminu Idris. "Our mission is to cultivate the next generation of cybersecurity leaders who can not only defend against AI-driven threats but also leverage AI to create more resilient security postures. This program is the culmination of years of front-line experience, and it is built to deliver job-ready experts from day one."

Mr. Idris brings a wealth of experience to the program, holding a formidable array of top-tier industry certifications, including CCNA, CompTIA Security+, CEH, OSCP, and CISSP. His role as a cybersecurity educator and mentor has shaped a curriculum that is both academically rigorous and intensely practical.

Key features of the **Advanced AI in Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics** program include:

*   **Comprehensive 12-Month Curriculum:** 52 weeks of intensive study covering defensive AI, offensive AI, AI-powered forensics, and AI governance.
*   **12 Production-Ready Labs:** Complete with over 5,000 lines of Python code, real-world datasets, and step-by-step manuals.
*   **Expert-Crafted Content:** The entire program, from its strategic framework to the code in its labs, was developed by Aminu Idris, ensuring a cohesive and expert-driven learning experience.
*   **Career-Focused Outcomes:** The program is directly aligned with high-demand career roles such as AI Security Specialist, Adversarial AI Researcher, and Forensic Data Scientist, which command salaries ranging from $120,000 to over $200,000.

Enrollment is now open for the inaugural cohort, which is set to begin on September 1, 2026. The program is offered in a hybrid format to accommodate both local and international students.

**About the International Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics Academy (ICDFA):**
The ICDFA is a premier institution dedicated to providing advanced, hands-on training for the next generation of cybersecurity and digital forensics professionals. Founded by industry expert Aminu Idris, the academy is committed to excellence, innovation, and producing graduates with verifiable, real-world skills.
