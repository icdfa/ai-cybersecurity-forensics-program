**Subject: Invitation to Apply: Fully Funded Fellowship in Advanced AI Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics**

**Dear [Applicant Name],**

On behalf of the International Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics Academy (ICDFA), I am honored to invite you to apply for the inaugural cohort of our **Advanced AI in Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics Fellowship**.

This prestigious 12-month, fully funded program is designed to cultivate the next generation of elite AI security experts. Thanks to the generous sponsorship of the **International Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics Foundation**, we are able to offer this world-class training, valued at over $15,000, at no cost to the selected fellows.

**The Opportunity:**

The rapid rise of AI-powered cyberattacks has created an unprecedented demand for professionals who can operate at the intersection of artificial intelligence and cybersecurity. This intensive program was meticulously crafted to address this critical skills gap, providing deep, hands-on expertise that traditional academic programs cannot offer.

**Program Highlights:**

*   **Fully Funded:** 100% of tuition costs are covered by the ICDF Foundation.
*   **Exclusive Cohort:** Admission is limited to a maximum of **50 fellows** to ensure a low student-to-instructor ratio and personalized mentorship.
*   **Comprehensive Curriculum:** 52 weeks of intensive training covering Offensive AI, Defensive AI, AI-Powered Forensics, and AI Governance.
*   **Unrivaled Resources:** Fellows receive free access to enterprise-grade tools and platforms through our partnerships with **Microsoft (Azure & Learn), AWS Academy, Cisco, and GitHub**.

**Application Timeline:**

*   **Program Start Date:** September 1, 2026

This is a career-defining opportunity for individuals with a strong passion for cybersecurity and a drive to master the technologies that will shape the future of digital defense. We are seeking the most promising candidates to join this merit-based, global cohort.
