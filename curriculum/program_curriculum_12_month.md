# Advanced AI in Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics Fellowship - Curriculum v4.0

## Program Philosophy

This 12-month, fully funded fellowship is an intensive, hands-on program designed to cultivate the next generation of elite AI security and forensics experts. This is a deep, immersive experience focused on practical application, hands-on labs with enterprise-grade tools, and contribution to real-world security challenges.

## Program Structure: 2 Phases, 24 Modules

The curriculum is divided into two intensive 6-month phases, comprising a total of 24 advanced modules.

--- 

### Phase 1: Core Skills & Defensive Operations (Months 1-6)

**Objective:** Build a world-class foundation in advanced cybersecurity, cloud operations, and defensive AI, fully leveraging our industry partnerships from day one.

*   **Module 1:** Fellowship Orientation & Professional Development
*   **Module 2:** Advanced Python for Security Automation
*   **Module 3:** Enterprise Networking & Security Architecture (Cisco Partner Curriculum)
*   **Module 4:** Cloud Security Engineering (AWS Academy & Microsoft Azure Labs)
*   **Module 5:** Applied Machine Learning for Security Professionals
*   **Module 6:** Deep Learning for Threat Detection
*   **Module 7:** AI-Powered Threat Intelligence & Proactive Threat Hunting
*   **Module 8:** Intelligent Malware Analysis & Reverse Engineering
*   **Module 9:** AI for Advanced Network Defense & Intrusion Detection
*   **Module 10:** AI-Enhanced Digital Forensics & Large-Scale Investigation
*   **Module 11:** Natural Language Processing for Security (Log Analysis, Phishing Detection)
*   **Module 12:** AI-Driven Security Orchestration (SOAR) & Automation

--- 

### Phase 2: Offensive AI, Advanced Security & Capstone Project (Months 7-12)

**Objective:** Pivot to an offensive mindset to understand and mitigate next-generation threats, culminating in a significant, real-world capstone project.

*   **Module 13:** AI Ethics, Governance & Legal Frameworks
*   **Module 14:** Adversarial Machine Learning: Theory & Practice
*   **Module 15:** Red Teaming & Security Validation of AI Systems
*   **Module 16:** Generative AI Threats & Deepfake Forensics
*   **Module 17:** AI for Vulnerability Research & Exploit Development
*   **Module 18:** SOC Simulation & Team-Based Incident Response
*   **Module 19:** Research Seminar I: State-of-the-Art in AI Security
*   **Module 20:** Mid-Program Research Project & Presentation
*   **Module 21:** Capstone Project I: Proposal & Initial Research
*   **Module 22:** Capstone Project II: Development & Implementation
*   **Module 23:** Capstone Project III: Final Report & Industry Presentation
*   **Module 24:** Career Development & Placement
