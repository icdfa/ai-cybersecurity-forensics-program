# Curriculum

This directory contains all curriculum materials for the 12-month program.

## Documents

- **program_curriculum_12_month.md** - High-level curriculum overview with quarterly breakdown
- **detailed_curriculum_weekly.md** - Week-by-week detailed breakdown of all 52 weeks
- **assessment_framework.md** - Complete assessment strategy and grading rubrics
- **sample_quiz_q1_m1.md** - Sample quiz for Quarter 1, Month 1 with answer key

## Structure

The curriculum is organized into 4 quarters, each lasting 3 months (12 weeks), for a total of 52 weeks of intensive study.

### Quarter 1: Foundations of AI and Cybersecurity
- Introduction to Python for cybersecurity
- Fundamentals of AI and machine learning
- Threat intelligence and threat prediction
- Malware analysis with AI

### Quarter 2: AI-Powered Digital Forensics and Incident Response
- Digital forensics principles
- AI for evidence analysis
- Timeline reconstruction
- SOAR and incident response automation

### Quarter 3: Offensive AI and Adversarial Machine Learning
- Adversarial AI threat landscape
- Evasion and poisoning attacks
- Deepfakes and generative AI threats
- Red teaming and security validation

### Quarter 4: AI Governance, Capstone, and Career Prep
- AI risk management frameworks
- Ethical hacking and responsible disclosure
- Capstone project development
- Career preparation and professional development

## Assessment Methods

The program uses multiple assessment methods:
- Lab assignments (40%)
- Quizzes (15%)
- Mini-projects (20%)
- Midterm exam (10%)
- Final capstone project (15%)
